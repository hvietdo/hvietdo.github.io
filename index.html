About me

My full name is Hoang Viet Do (and Đỗ Hoàng Việt in Vietnamese). I am a former Master's student at Hanoi Institute of Mathematics (IMH) and was supervised by Professor Ngo Viet Trung. You can find my curriculum vitae and my thesis. My interests include (Algebraic and Geometric) Combinatorics, Commutative Algebra, (Algebraic and Fractal) Geometry and Probability.

Prior to my Master's studies, I studied at the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science, VNUHCM-University of Science where I started mathematical research under the guidance of Professor Mai Hoang Bien. You can find my senior dissertation here.
