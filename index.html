About me

My full name is Hoang Viet Do (and Đỗ Hoàng Việt in Vietnamese). I am currently a Master's student at Hanoi Institute of Mathematics (IMH). I am supervised by Professor Ngo Viet Trung conducting researches on Combinatorial Commutative Algebra. You can find my curriculum vitae at here.

Prior to Master's period, I studied at Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science, VNUHCM-University of Science where I researched Group and Graph Theory under the guidance of Associate Professor Mai Hoang Bien. You can find my senior dissertation here.
