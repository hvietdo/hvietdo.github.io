About me

My full name is Hoang Viet Do (and Đỗ Hoàng Việt in Vietnamese). I am currently a third-year student at Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science, VNUHCM-University of Science where I am fortunate to be supervised by Professor Mai Hoang Bien. You can find my curriculum vitae at here. My interests include the Group theory and Representation theory, specifically the Character theory of finite groups.

In Spring 2021, I have co-run a seminar on the theory of characters of finite groups.
